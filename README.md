# Coding Challenges DSA Bot 🤖

Welcome to the **Coding Challenges DSA Bot** for Discord! This bot aids in enhancing your data structure and algorithm skills right within Discord. By sourcing coding challenges from LeetCode, it offers you and your server members a novel challenge daily.

![LeetCode Logo](https://leetcode.com/_next/static/images/logo-dark-c96c407d175e36c81e236fcfdd682a0b.png)

## 🌟 Features

### 1. Scheduled Challenges 📆
Every day, the bot reliably posts challenges in the "code-challenges" channel:
   - 🌅 **6:00 AM** - An Easy challenge to initiate your morning
   - 🌞 **12:00 PM** - A Medium challenge for a midday brain teaser
   - 🌙 **6:00 PM** - A Hard challenge to stretch your coding muscles in the evening

### 2. Personalized Challenge Requests 🎯
With a straightforward command, users can ask for challenges tailored to their tastes:
   - 🔓 **Free or Paid Challenges**: Specify according to your preference!
   - 🌡️ **Difficulty Levels**: Opt between Easy, Medium, or Hard.

The bot sends the challenge to a user-specific private channel, ensuring an individualized experience.

### 3. Private Channels for Members 🚪
Upon a new member's arrival, the bot makes them feel special! It spawns a private channel exclusively for the newcomer, guaranteeing a zone for personalized interactions and challenge dispatches.
